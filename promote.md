## My recommendation (important)

Be careful with phrases like:

> “auto create Facebook accounts”
> “mass add members”
> “scrape private groups”

These can sound risky and may trigger platform trust issues if shown publicly.

For public promotion:
Use softer wording like:

* Audience growth tools
* Community management tools
* Lead generation systems
* Marketing automation

Keep the more aggressive features for private sales calls/demo discussions.
